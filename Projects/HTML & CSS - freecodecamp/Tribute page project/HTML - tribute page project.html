Solskjær Returns

The former striker has breathed new life into the team, with three players in particular shining under his stewardship.

A positive impact

Solskjær must surely be in with a shot at the role full-time after his fast start to life as the boss at Old Trafford.

  • A 100% win-rate from his first 8 games in charge is the best start of any Manchester United Manager ever.
  • It's also the most successful start to a Premier League manager job, surpassing Pep Guardiola.
  • Restoring a fast, dynamic system the team find themselves full of goals at the moment.
  • Pogba in particular is rejuvinated, with 4 goals and 4 assists so far under Solskjær.
  • The fans say he's "made football fun again".
